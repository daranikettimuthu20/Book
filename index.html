Darani Kettimuthu

Storyteller • Educator • Creator of Toddlers Twist

Darani Kettimuthu is a storyteller, educator, and creative soul who believes every child carries a spark of imagination waiting to shine. Her books are crafted with warmth, curiosity, and a deep love for helping young learners explore the world with confidence and joy.

From playful alphabet adventures to heartwarming stories about kindness, science, and discovery, Darani’s work celebrates the magic of childhood—where learning feels like play and every page opens a new door.

As a mother, mentor, and lifelong learner, she brings patience, creativity, and purpose to everything she creates. Her mission is simple and powerful: to inspire little minds, nurture big dreams, and make learning a joyful journey for every child.
